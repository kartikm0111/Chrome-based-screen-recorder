Built for creators, engineers & educators

Screen + microphone

Records any screen, window, or tab, and mixes your microphone in real-time.

Pause & resume

Take a breath, adjust the scene, and pick right back up where you left off.

Instant preview

Scrub through your capture the moment you stop — no round-trip required.

One-click download

Exports a self-contained WebM file with a smart, timestamped filename.

Privacy first

Everything happens locally in your browser. No server, no analytics, no tracking.

Live duration

A high-precision timer keeps track of your recording length, pauses aware.

Webcam overlay

Burn a circular face-cam bubble into any corner of the recording — composited live.

In-browser trim

Cut the dead air off both ends of your clip before downloading — no editor needed.

Screenshot mode

Grab a full-resolution PNG still of the shared screen mid-recording.

How it works

  1. 1

    Pick your sources

    Toggle microphone and system audio, and pick a video quality preset.

  2. 2

    Grant screen access

    Chrome will ask which screen, window, or tab to share — pick and confirm.

  3. 3

    Record, pause, resume

    A live timer and REC badge keep you in the loop while you work.

  4. 4

    Preview & download

    Watch it back instantly and export a WebM file to your Downloads folder.
